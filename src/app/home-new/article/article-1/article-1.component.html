Euro rose against the US dollar

This happened to the closing of US sales on Tuesday, 28th of December. EUR/USD is trading at 1.0457, adding 0.03%. Also, the euro rose against the British pound and the Japanese yen. EUR/GBP has reached a level of 0,8522 and EUR/JPY has reached a level of 122,81.
